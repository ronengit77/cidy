Application Design

1

Capture

The conversation starts with a welcome, then UserInquiry2 captures the first question and initializes transcript state.

2

Classify

The classifier sets normalized global variables for knowledge domain, funding stream, topic area, clarification need, and status.

3

Clarify

The clarifier asks targeted closed-list questions when the classifier needs fund, domain, or artifact context.

4

Route

The router sends DA and RPTC requests to response topics, routes out-of-scope or dead-end states to feedback, and currently stops some recognized domains as not developed.

5

Answer

Response topics search selected knowledge sources and write Global.draftResponse with answer, sources, and confidence.

6

Close the loop

Confidence formatting, warning, sharing, feedback, escalation, restart, and end-of-conversation topics complete the interaction lifecycle.
